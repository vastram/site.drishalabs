Drape.it – Virtual try-on reimagined

Drape.it is our flagship virtual try-on platform that lets shoppers see how outfits would look and move on real bodies—before they ever step into a fitting room. By combining advanced body understanding, cloth behavior, and photoreal rendering, Drape.it brings an in-store fitting experience to any screen.

  • Increase engagement – Make browsing feel like play with immersive, interactive try-ons.
  • Reduce returns – Help shoppers make confident choices with more realistic previews.
  • On-brand experiences – Tailored user journeys and visuals that match your brand.
  • Built to scale – Designed for modern retail catalogs and high-traffic launches.
